👤 User Details
Manage all system users. Assign roles to control access levels — Administrator has full access, while User has limited access based on permissions.
📖 Role Hierarchy — How It Works
👑 Administrator
Top level access. Can access ALL modules — Export, Import, EXIM Marketing, Admin Master, User Management, and all Settings. Like the CEO of the company who has access to everything.
🔑 Admin
Senior access. Can access most modules and manage users below their level. Cannot access Admin Master settings. Like an HR Manager who manages staff but doesn't control company policy.
📊 Manager
Department access. Can access modules assigned to their department. Can view reports but cannot create or delete master data. Like a department head who manages their team's work.
👥 User
Basic access. Can only use modules specifically permitted to them. Cannot manage other users or access Admin settings. Like an employee who only does their assigned tasks.
