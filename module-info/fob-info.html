What is FOB?

Free On Board — Your Cost Until the Ship

FOB (Free On Board) is one of the most widely used Incoterms in international trade. It means the seller is responsible for all costs and risks until the goods are loaded on board the vessel at the named port of shipment. After that, the buyer takes responsibility.

To quote an accurate FOB price to your buyer, you must know every single expense involved in getting your goods from your factory to the ship's deck — including port handling, documentation, customs clearance, freight to port, and all other local charges.

IMPEXIO's Container Expenses FOB module captures all 16 standard expense line items across four container types — 20', 40' GP, 40' HQ, and LCL — and automatically calculates your Total Expenses and Per CBM Cost for each container type.

Expense Items

All 16 FOB Expense Line Items

Every standard charge involved in an Indian FOB shipment is covered. Enter values for each container type — totals calculate automatically.

🏗️
THC & Docs

Terminal Handling Charges and documentation fees charged by the shipping line at the port of loading.

📄
B/L Charges

Bill of Lading issuance fee charged by the shipping line. Required for every container movement.

📋
Shipping Bill

Government fee for filing the Shipping Bill — mandatory export customs document in India.

💻
EDI

Electronic Data Interchange charges for customs filing through ICEGATE — the Indian customs portal.

🏢
Agency

CHA (Customs House Agent) or freight forwarder agency fees for handling export documentation.

🚛
On Wheel

Transportation charges for moving the container from the factory or CFS to the port of loading.

🔍
Customs Expense

Miscellaneous customs handling and examination charges levied at the port during export clearance.

🌍
Certificate of Origin

Fees for obtaining a Certificate of Origin — required for preferential duty benefits in destination country.

🚨
Transportation for Hazara

Special transportation charges applicable for hazardous or heavy-weight cargo requiring special handling.

🛡️
Insurance at Actual

Marine cargo insurance premium from the warehouse to the port. Entered at actual cost incurred.

📝
First Time Export Reg.

One-time registration charges for first-time exporters with DGFT or other government bodies.

🌿
Fumigation Charges

Mandatory fumigation treatment charges for agricultural and food products — required by importing countries.

🔒
Seal Charges

Container seal and locking charges applied by the shipping line or CFS at the time of stuffing.

⚖️
Weighment Charges

VGM (Verified Gross Mass) weighment charges — mandatory under SOLAS regulations for all containers.

📦
Miscellaneous

Any other incidental charges not covered above — port labour, overtime, inspection fees etc.

💹
Per CBM Cost (Auto)

Automatically calculated as Total Expenses ÷ CBM. Shows your exact cost per cubic metre for each container type.
